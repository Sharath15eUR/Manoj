Difference between 802.11a and 802.11b:

802.11a :
->It operates at 5 GHz, provides faster speeds (54 Mbps) but has a shorter range.
->Shorter range compared to 802.11b due to higher frequency.
->Less prone to interference from other devices operating on 2.4 GHz
.
802.11b:
->It operates at 2.4 GHz, offers lower speeds (11 Mbps) but has a longer range and more interference.
->Longer range compared to 802.11a due to lower frequency.
->More prone to interference from other devices operating on 2.4 GHz.

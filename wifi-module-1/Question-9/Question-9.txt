Types of Wi-Fi Internet Connectivity Backhaul & Their Use Cases:


1. DSL (Digital Subscriber Line) Backhaul: 
	->Commonly used for home broadband, small office setups, and connecting rural areas to the internet.

2. Cable (Coaxial) Backhaul:
	-> Used for residential broadband services and within ISP networks.

3. 4G LTE Backhaul: 

	->Fiber Optic Backhaul: Provides high-speed internet in urban areas, business environments, and data centers.

4. Ethernet Backhaul: 
	->Preferred for enterprise Wi-Fi networks, office infrastructures, and campus access point connections.


5. Mesh Wi-Fi Backhaul: 
	->Best suited for large homes, offices, and campuses requiring seamless, wide-area Wi-Fi coverage.

6. Power Line Communication Backhaul: 
	->Utilizes existing electrical wiring to extend internet connectivity throughout homes and offices
